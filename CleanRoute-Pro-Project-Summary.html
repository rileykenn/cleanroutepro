Executive Summary

CleanRoute Pro is a cloud-based scheduling, rostering, and operations platform built specifically for The Cleaning Co Shellharbour. The application is substantially complete; every major feature from both the original scope ($6,500) and the Phase 2 amendment ($500) has been built and is functional.

The platform is live and accessible on any device via web browser. Below is a detailed breakdown of every feature area, what has been delivered, recent improvements, and what remains.

Summary

CleanRoute Pro is a 27,500+ line, custom-built platform covering all features from both the original scope of work and the Phase 2 amendment. The platform handles daily schedule planning with live route calculation, multi-team management, staff rostering with role-based access, a full-featured checklist/form builder with 13 field types, automated payroll calculations, and comprehensive data export across 6 formats.

The system is live, cloud-hosted, and accessible from any device. Staff can view their daily schedules, complete checklists with photo/video evidence, and navigate to jobs via Google Maps, all from their phones.

The remaining items to complete are: Stripe subscription billing setup, custom domain configuration, server-side email delivery for checklists, and onboarding documentation.
