1Context

This document is an amendment to the original CleanRoute Pro Scope of Work (v4.0, dated 24 April 2026). It covers two additional features requested during the testing phase that fall outside the original scope.

Original Scope Reference: Section 2.10 (Checklists) covered basic tick-off checklist templates with per-client customisation, staff mobile view, completion tracking, and email reporting. The features below expand the checklist system into a full form builder with advanced field types, conditional logic, and media uploads. The staff payroll export is a new feature not covered in the original scope.

2Additional Features

2.1 Form & Checklist Builder

A comprehensive form builder that extends the existing checklist system into a flexible, mobile-first tool for on-site job reporting. Admins create templates with multiple field types; staff complete them on their phones during jobs.

Feature Description
Form Structure Heading/section titles with description fields. Sections and questions can be reordered via drag and drop
Field Types Open text input, Yes/No toggle, single-select dropdown, multi-select dropdown, date picker, time picker, image upload, and video upload
Conditional Logic Show or hide follow-up questions based on Yes/No responses (e.g. "Are there ceiling fans?" → If YES → "Dust ceiling fans thoroughly")
Custom Dropdowns Admin-defined dropdown options with support for multiple selection where needed
Required Fields Mark any field as required so staff cannot submit the form without completing it
Not Applicable Option Optional "N/A" toggle on fields so staff can mark items as not applicable for a particular visit
Master Templates Save, duplicate, and manage reusable form templates. Assign templates to specific clients for repeated use
Pre-filled Fields Current date/time, staff name, and client name/address auto-populated from the booking when a form is opened
Photo & Video Uploads Staff can attach photos and videos directly to form submissions from their mobile device
Auto-Save Drafts In-progress forms are auto-saved so staff don't lose work if they navigate away or lose connection briefly
Mobile-First Design Optimised for fast on-site completion on mobile devices — large touch targets, smooth inputs, and minimal scrolling
Email Report Completed forms can be automatically emailed to the client and/or management as a job completion report
PDF Export Export any completed form as a downloadable PDF document for record keeping, client reporting, or printing

2.2 Staff Weekly Payroll Export

A dedicated view to isolate a single staff member's full week of work, providing all the data needed to process payroll without switching between days or teams.

Feature Description
Staff Isolation View Select any staff member and view their complete week at a glance — which teams they were on, which jobs they worked, and when
Daily Breakdown Per-day summary showing start time, finish time, jobs completed, travel time, and travel distance
Weekly Totals Aggregated totals for the week: total hours worked, total travel time, total distance, and total breaks
Wage Calculation Automatic wage output using the staff member's individual hourly rate. Displayed in both standard format (e.g. 38h 15m) and decimal format (e.g. 38.25 hours) for payroll compatibility
Allowances Per-kilometre and fuel allowance calculations included in the weekly summary
CSV Export Export the staff member's weekly summary as a downloadable CSV file for easy import into payroll systems

3Not Included in This Amendment

The following were noted as future enhancements and are not part of this build:

  • Offline mode for form completion
  • Digital signature capture fields
  • Audit/history logs for form submissions
  • Advanced escalation workflows or notifications
  • Internal vs. client-visible note separation
  • Direct Xero integration
